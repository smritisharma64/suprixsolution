QA & Testing

Quality Assurance (QA) testing, often referred to simply as QA, is a critical process within software development and IT consulting. Its primary goal is to ensure that software applications and systems meet predefined quality standards and perform as expected before they are released to end-users or deployed in production environments. QA testing involves various activities and methodologies to identify defects, assess functionality, and validate software against business requirements..

Importance of QA Testing:..

Quality Assurance:-

Ensures that software meets quality standards and performs reliably in real-world environments..

Risk Mitigation:-

Identifies and addresses defects early in the development lifecycle, reducing the likelihood of issues impacting end-users..

Customer Satisfaction:-

Improves user experience by delivering software that functions as expected, meeting user requirements and expectations..

Cost Savings:-

Reduces the cost of fixing defects post-release and avoids potential revenue loss due to software failures..
